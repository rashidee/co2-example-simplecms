Getting Started with Simple CMS

Published on March 10, 2026 · 8 min read

Back to Blog

Welcome to Simple CMS, the easiest way for small business owners to create professional marketing pages and manage blog content. In this guide, we will walk you through everything you need to know to get your first page live in under 30 minutes.

Why Choose Simple CMS?

Running a small business means wearing many hats. You should not have to become a web developer just to have an online presence. Simple CMS was built from the ground up with non-technical users in mind. Our intuitive interface lets you focus on what matters most: your business.

Whether you are a bakery owner, a freelance consultant or a local services provider, Simple CMS gives you the tools to look professional online without the complexity or cost of custom web development.

Step 1: Set Up Your Account

Getting started is straightforward. Visit our sign-up page and create your account with just your email address and a password. You will receive a confirmation email within seconds. Once confirmed, you are ready to start building.

Step 2: Choose Your Sections

Simple CMS comes with pre-built sections that you can customise to match your brand. Here are the sections available:

  • Hero Section — A full-width carousel to showcase your key messages with compelling imagery and calls-to-action.
  • Products & Services — Display what you offer with images, descriptions and links to learn more.
  • Features — Highlight the key benefits of working with you using icons and short descriptions.
  • Testimonials — Build trust by showcasing reviews from your satisfied customers.
  • Team — Introduce the people behind your business with photos and LinkedIn profiles.
  • Contact — Make it easy for visitors to reach you with a built-in contact form.

Step 3: Customise Your Content

Each section comes with sample content that you can replace with your own. Upload your images, write your copy and adjust colours to match your brand. The admin portal makes it simple to edit text, swap images and rearrange elements.

Our design system ensures that everything looks polished and consistent. You do not need to worry about fonts, spacing or alignment. Simple CMS handles the design so you can focus on your message.

Step 4: Start Your Blog

A blog is one of the most effective ways to drive organic traffic to your website. With Simple CMS, you can create, categorise and publish blog posts in minutes. Use tags and categories to organise your content, and let visitors search by keywords.

Step 5: Publish and Share

Once you are happy with your content, hit publish. Your marketing page and blog will be live and accessible to visitors immediately. Share your new site on social media, include it in your email signature and start driving traffic to your business.

What Comes Next?

This is just the beginning. As your business grows, you can add more products, publish new blog posts and collect more testimonials. Simple CMS scales with you, so you will never outgrow your website.

If you have any questions or need help getting started, visit our contact page or reach out to our customer success team. We are here to help you succeed online.
